def rfm_segment(row):
    if row['R_Score'] == '5' and row['F_Score'] == '5' and row['M_Score'] == '5':
        return 'Champions'
    elif row['R_Score'] >= '4' and row['F_Score'] >= '4':
        return 'Loyal Customers'
    elif row['R_Score'] >= '4' and row['F_Score'] <= '2':
        return 'Potential Loyalists'
    elif row['R_Score'] <= '2' and row['F_Score'] >= '4':
        return 'At Risk'
    elif row['R_Score'] == '1' and row['F_Score'] == '1':
        return 'Lost'
    else:
        return 'Others'

Interpretation of Results¶

Interpretation of Results:

  1. The ‘Others’ segment contains the highest number of customers, indicating a large base of low to medium engagement customers.

  2. Champions and Loyal Customers, although fewer in number, show a higher average monetary value, making them the most valuable customers.

  3. At Risk customers have moderate frequency but lower recency, suggesting they may stop purchasing if not targeted.

  4. Lost customers show low engagement across all RFM metrics and contribute least to revenue.

Business Recommendations¶

Business Recommendations:

  1. Focus marketing efforts on Champions and Loyal Customers through exclusive offers and loyalty programs.

  2. Convert Potential Loyalists into loyal customers using targeted promotions.

  3. Re-engage At Risk customers with discounts or reminders.

  4. Avoid spending marketing budget on Lost customers.

Conclusion¶

This project successfully segmented customers based on their purchasing behavior using RFM analysis. The analysis helped identify high-value customers as well as customers who are at risk of loosing them. Such segmentation enables businesses to design targeted marketing strategies, improve customer retention, and increase overall revenue.
